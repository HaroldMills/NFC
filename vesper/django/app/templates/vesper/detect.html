Detect

Runs the Old Bird Tseep and/or Thrush detectors on one or more recordings. The recordings are specified as a set of one or more stations and a range of dates. Due to a limitation of the Old Bird detectors, the recordings are processed one at a time. If more than one detector is selected, however, the detectors can run simultaneously on a given recording. Detection runs as a Vesper job.
